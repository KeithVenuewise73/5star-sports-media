What Is The Academy?

Learn by doing.

The 5-Star Sports Media Academy trains high school and college students in sports journalism, photography, interviewing, broadcasting, public relations, and digital content creation — while giving them real-world experience covering Western New York amateur sports.

Academy members cover games, write stories, interview athletes and coaches, take photos, create digital content, and build a real media portfolio — the kind that actually matters when applying to colleges, internships, and careers.

5-Star Sports Media Academy gives aspiring young media professionals the opportunity to learn, create, publish, and grow — while helping recognize the athletes, coaches, teams, organizations, and community leaders who make Western New York amateur sports special.

Core Mission
Recognize athletes.
Develop storytellers.
Build futures.

Every athlete, coach, and team in WNY has a story worth telling. The Academy trains the next generation of storytellers to tell them — and builds their own future in the process.

The Mentor Model

Guided by professionals.
Built on real experience.

Academy students receive direct guidance from experienced media professionals, photographers, public relations experts, business leaders, and community mentors who have been in the rooms and on the sidelines our students are working toward.

This isn't a lecture. Mentors bring real feedback, real connections, and real-world perspective to every track — so students learn how things actually work, not just how textbooks say they should.
