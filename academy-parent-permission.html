Program Overview

The 5-Star Sports Media Academy is a hands-on training program for high school and college students interested in sports journalism, photography, broadcasting, public relations, digital content, or sports business. Students earn real bylines, build professional portfolios, and receive mentor guidance from media and community professionals.

Student Expectations
Follow the Academy Code of Conduct at all assignments
Complete track assignments with honesty and accuracy
Communicate promptly with mentors and editors
Represent 5-Star Sports Media professionally
Report any concerns to an adult mentor immediately
Media Assignment Expectations
Game coverage, athlete interviews, and photo assignments at WNY sporting events
All field assignments are structured and mentor-guided
Student work will be reviewed editorially before publication
Published content will appear on 5starsportsmedia.com with the student's byline
Safety Guidelines
No unsupervised or private interviews with minors
All field assignments take place at public sporting events
Academy mentors are available for all field assignments
Students are not permitted to attend events outside their assignment
Transportation to and from assignments is the responsibility of the family
