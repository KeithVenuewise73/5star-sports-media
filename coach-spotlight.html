Coach Brian S. — Twenty Years, Thousands of Kids, Zero Quit

Coach Philosophy

Every player walks into the building as a kid and walks out as a competitor, a teammate, and a person ready to handle something hard. That's the program.

Impact on Athletes

Hundreds of former players still reach out to Coach Brian when life gets hard. Three of his former players now coach youth programs themselves — because of what he did for them.

"I'm not coaching pucks. I'm coaching people. Hockey is just how we do it."
