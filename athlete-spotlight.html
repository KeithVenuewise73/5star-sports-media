Girls Lacrosse • East High School

Jenna P. — The Leader Everyone Wanted on Their Team

From leading her travel lacrosse team to back-to-back tournament titles to being voted captain by her peers, Jenna's story isn't just about goals and assists — it's about character, grit, and making everyone around her better.

A three-year varsity starter and two-time All-Section honoree, Jenna also volunteers weekly at a youth lacrosse program in the city where she grew up. "I want to be the person for these kids that someone else was for me," she says.
