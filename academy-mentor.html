Time Commitment

Mentor involvement is flexible. Options range from a single guest speaker session to ongoing one-on-one track mentorship. We work around your schedule — not the other way around.

Most mentors contribute 1-3 hours per month. We never overbook your time — you decide what you can give.

Mentor Roles

Find your role in the Academy.

Mentors can contribute in whatever way fits their expertise and availability.

🎤
Guest Speaker

A single session — share your story, your career path, and what you know. Powerful, low-commitment, and often the moment a student figures out what they want to do.

✍️
Writing Mentor

Review student stories and recaps, give editorial feedback, and help reporters develop their voice and craft over their track assignment cycle.

📸
Photography Mentor

Review student photo galleries, teach technique in the field, and help photographers understand how to see a story through a lens.

🎙️
Interview & Broadcast Mentor

Coach students on interview preparation, on-camera presence, and how to deliver a compelling segment or podcast episode.

📣
PR & Communications Mentor

Guide students through press releases, organization stories, and sponsor communications — the professional writing skills that translate to every career.

💼
Business & Leadership Mentor

Help students in the Sports Business track understand how careers are built, how sponsorships work, and what Beyond The Game really looks like.
